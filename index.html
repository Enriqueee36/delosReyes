Accountancy, Business and Management Track

The Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM) strand, The ABM strand is designed to equip students with skills and knowledge in various areas of business, including accounting, finance, entrepreneurship, marketing, and management. It aims to prepare students for careers in the business industry and to develop their ability to analyze business problems and make informed decisions.

Humanities and Social Sciences Track

The Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) strand, is designed to equip students with a broad range of knowledge in the areas of social sciences, humanities, and communication. It aims to develop their critical thinking and analytical skills, their ability to understand human behavior and society, and their communication and research skills. t provides them with a strong foundation in these areas, which they can use to further their education or jumpstart their career.

Information and Communications Technology Track

Information and Communication Technology(ICT), The ICT strand is designed to equip students with skills and knowledge in various areas of technology, including software development, programming, networking, multimedia, and web design. It aims to prepare students for careers in the rapidly growing technology industry and to develop their ability to use technology as a tool to solve real-world problems.

Agri-Fishery Arts Track

Agri-Fishery Arts Strand (AFA), This strand was designed for hands-on learning and application of agricultural and aquaculture skills. It is intended to provide students with information and abilities in agriculture, fishing, and other related businesses. The strand is designed to educate students with the knowledge and skills needed to become successful entrepreneurs and professionals in the agri-fishery sector.
